Zero baseline: bars vs lines

This page is a small interactive explainer inspired by an article by Enrico Bertini on using focused, interactive examples to teach data visualization concepts.

The explainer focuses on the role of the y-axis baseline in bar charts and line charts. Its goal is to show how changing the axis minimum can dramatically alter the visual impression of the data, and why careful axis design is essential to avoid misleading interpretations.
